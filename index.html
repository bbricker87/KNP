About Me

I'm a natural light photographer based in Central Ohio. I'm a college student (O-H!) passionate about photography and would love to share my passion with you to create photos and portraits that you will love for years to come.

Please feel free to take a look around my site and at my portfolio to get a feel for my photography style. I try to mix candid moments with both creative and traditional pose photos. I love capturing moments for you & your family, and that's why I do what I do.

Thanks for visiting my website! I appreciate your support and hope I get to meet and work with you soon!
